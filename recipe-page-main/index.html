Simple Omplette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette beaten eggs cooked to perfect, optinally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetable, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation: 5 minutes

  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings cheesen diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
